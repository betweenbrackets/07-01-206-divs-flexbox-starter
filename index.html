As I recently drove the Alcan and landed in the Seattle area, you will find me making the most of my non-monitor time hiking the Kitsap and Olympic Peninsulas. My friends have dubbed this, "getting my Alaska on." Being at play in the huge, huge trees centers and inspires me, and makes me a little less homesick.

My passion (second to problem-solving): I LOVE all things music. Singing. Playing. Recording. Theory. Concerts.

A fun fact: In my rural Alaska life, I built my house. The directions from my house to the big box stores: head out of town, pass the flashing light, take the first paved left, and the second light on your right. A distance of about 300 miles. On a two lane road. Watching for moose.
